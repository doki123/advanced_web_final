Can I request changes to a drink?

Though we make a point to be as open and accomodating to our cherished customers as possible, we do have some limitations as to drink requests. You can always request the amount of pulp/seeds/chunks in a drink, and some ingredients can be replaced in case of allergies. However, some elements of our drink are needed for an optimal drink and cannot be replaced.

Can I reuse my coupon?

Your coupons can be used at any location, and will not expire. However, those coupons are one-use only, and cannot be re-used. You can get a free coupon by signing up to our mailing list here.

Do you cater for events?

We cater for all types of events, from casual lunches to business conferences to large formal balls. We will need at least three business day's worth of notice before an event, and we reserve the right to refuse requests at our discretion. Last minute proposals will be liable to higher charging rates. You can contact us for catering requests here.

How do you make your drinks?

We've all seen how you tend to get drinks in most restaurants, right? Just pre-prepared empty calories from who-knows-what source, carelessly poured into a paper cup and served. We here at Jumpin' Juice Co, though, make sure that all our drinks are crafted in real time, right in front of you! We strive to be as transparent as possible, and that extends to the actual creation of our drinks too. Our employees take pride in their work, and you can be sure to be impressed by their efficiency and flair while creating your beverage!
