Mixed Berry Smoothie

This tart smoothie is made of pomegranates, raspberries, passionfruit, and strawberries blended together with hints of vanilla extract to mellow out the tang. Mixed with blueberry crush, this smoothie is hand-crafted for the long days of summer!

Ingredients

  • Passionfruit
  • Fresh Pomegranate
  • Raspberries
  • Sweet Strawberry
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Crushed Blueberry

Replaceable/Removeable Items

  • Vanilla Extract
