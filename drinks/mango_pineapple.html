Mango Pineapple Milkshake

This cold milkshake is perfect for the sweltering summer! Mango and pineapple are blended into one tropical flavor, with dashes of lemon and vanilla to add a pleasant tang. Scoops of creamy vanilla ice cream and whipped cream finish off this drink, creating the ideal escape from heat.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Mango
  • Fresh Pineapple
  • Squeezed Lemon Juice
  • Vanilla Extract
  • French Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Thick Whipped Cream

Replaceable/Removeable Items

  • Whipped Cream
  • Fresh Lemon Juice
