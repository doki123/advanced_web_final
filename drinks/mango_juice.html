Mango Lassi

Travel to the land of India with this substitute mango lassi straight out of Varanasi! This reimagining of the classic drink replaces the yoghurt with rich ice cream, creating a thick and creamy milkshake with a twist of mint.

Ingredients

  • Mango
  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Fresh Lemon Juice
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Mint
