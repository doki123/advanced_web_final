Cherry Lemon Soda

This sweet drink has a refreshing blend of cherries and lemon, with hints of mango, strawberry, and vanilla added for a full body flavor. Candied cherries and lemon rinds decorate the drink and infuse it with extra punch.

Ingredients

  • Crushed Cherry
  • Fresh Lemon Juice
  • Strawberry Blend
  • Sweet Mango Drizzle
  • Candied Masachino Cherry
  • Sugared Lemon Rind
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Fizzy Soda

Replaceable/Removeable Items

  • Candied Masachino Cherry
  • Pickled Lemon Rind
  • Mango Drizzle
