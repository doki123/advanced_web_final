Passionfruit Smoothie

This tangy smoothie is made from only the freshest ingredients--passionfruit, mango, apricot, and strawberries are chopped up and blended into a smooth, thick beverage. Lemon juice and vanilla extract are added for extra zest, with creamy vanilla ice cream mixed in as a finishing touch.

Ingredients

  • Passionfruit
  • Fresh Strawberry
  • Sweet Mango
  • Plump Apricot
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Lemon Juice
  • Vanilla Ice Cream

Replaceable/Removeable Items

  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Fresh Lemon Juice
  • Vanilla Extract
